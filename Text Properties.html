Iron Man

Iron Man is a iconic Marvel superhero, the alter ego of the brilliant billionaire, inventor, and philanthropist Tony Stark.

Created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby, he first appeared in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963. After being captured in a war zone and sustaining a severe heart injury, Stark uses his genius-level intellect to build a powered suit of armor to save his life and escape, later dedicating himself to protecting the world.

Famously portrayed by Robert Downey Jr. in the Marvel Cinematic Universe starting in 2008, he is a complex character defined by his evolution from a self-centered weapons manufacturer into a selfless hero, cementing his legacy with his ultimate sacrifice in Avengers: Endgame.
